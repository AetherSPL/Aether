The Percolator AMM
Every perpetuals exchange faces two unsolved fairness problems. The Percolator protocol addresses both with two independent mechanisms that compose cleanly — and require zero human intervention to maintain.
H
Fair Exit Mechanism
When the vault is stressed, every profitable account receives the same proportional haircut — determined by a single global ratio. No queue. No priority. No first-come advantage. Capital (deposits) is always senior and never touched.
h = min(Residual, PNL_matured) / PNL_matured

h = 1.0 → fully backed
h < 1.0 → pro-rata haircut
h rises automatically as residual recovers
A/K
Overhang Clearing
Two global coefficients per side replace the entire liquidation queue. When a position is liquidated, every counterparty absorbs the deficit proportionally. Settlement is O(1) per account, fully order-independent.
effective_pos(i) = floor(basis_i × A / a_basis_i)
pnl_delta(i) = floor(|basis_i| × (K − k_snap_i) / …)
